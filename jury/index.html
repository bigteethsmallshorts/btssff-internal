How to deliberate ▶ Show instructions
Welcome to Jury Deliberation — here's what to do
Below are two tabs: Micro Shorts and Small Shorts. Each tab shows the top contenders — films with the highest average scores from our judges — plus any wildcards, which are films that didn't make the top average but were a favorite for at least one judge. Every film deserves a fair look. Please reach out if you think a film should be added to this list for consideration.
What we need from you:  A ranking of your 1st, 2nd, and 3rd favorite films — and notes to help us deliberate.
Step 1 — Review Films
Click on each film to expand for more info. If you'd like to watch it again, hit ▶ Watch Film. Click "Show individual scores" to see every judge's previous ratings. Or feel free to ignore those and start with fresh eyes.
Step 2 — Add Notes
Write a deliberation note on each film and click Post to save it. Notes are visible to all jurors. Thoughtful notes help us make an informed decision — and positive ones may be shared with the filmmaker.
Step 3 — Rank Your Top 3
Use the #1 / #2 / #3 buttons to rank your top three films. Do this for both tabs — Micro Shorts AND Small Shorts.
Step 4 — Submit When Done
When you've ranked your top 3 in both categories, click I'm Done Voting at the bottom of the page. You can save your progress anytime and come back. When the button changes to "Voting Complete" — you're done! Thank you for contributing.
